| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Sales | 120M |

## Key Insights
- New York & California are top revenue states
- Street Footwear leads product category (37.35%)
- Q3 peak sales of 35M
- Online channel dominates at 37.4%
- West Gear is top retailer with 12M profit
